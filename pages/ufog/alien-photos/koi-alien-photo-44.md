*Koi Alien Photo 44*

Koi Alien Photo 44 appears on several websites.

One [popular UFO website (alienandufopictures.com)](http://www.alienandufopictures.com/alien_pictures_2.html)shows this photograph with the following caption: “The proof is starting to show that aliens have been visiting Earth for a very long time. This man in India discovered a skull which is clearly that of an alien that died sometime ago. Luckily, there was a camera around to have a picture of the alien head taken so that this discovery could be shared with the rest of the world. The Internet really is a beautiful thing that is bringing all of humanity closer together in our search for the truth of the universe.”

While the text above claims the skull was found in India, other websites contain claims (in various languages) that the image shows an American soldier in Vietnam with an alien skull.

Koi Alien Photo 44 was created by manipulating a photograph of a human skull to create a skull that appeared to be "alien".

Koi Alien Photo 44 appears on several websites.

The Internet really is a beautiful thing that is bringing all of humanity closer together in our search for the truth of the universe.”

While the text above claims the skull was found in India, other websites contain claims (in various languages) that the image shows an American soldier in Vietnam with an alien skull.

Koi Alien Photo 44 has also been used in connection with a story regarding alien skulls having been found during a restoration project at the Vatican Library. That story appeared on various websiites, including [a webpage on the ConjectureNew.com website](http://conjecturenews.com/aliens.htm)(which appears to have been the original source of this particular story) and also [on the GreatNews.com website](http://www.greatdreams.com/ufos/vatican.htm). Many alien skulls were found during a restoration project at the Vatican Library.

### 3. The real background to this image

Koi Alien Photo 44 was created by manipulating a photograph of a human skull to create a skull that appeared to be "alien".

The original (untouched) photograph is shown below (labelled "koi_ap_44_b").

A graphic designer commented in [a discussion on the AboveTopSecret.com discussion forum](http://www.abovetopsecret.com/forum/thread412944/pg1) that this image showed "quite possibly the worst use of the adobe photoshop liquify tool I have ever seen".

An article written in 2006 by Luis Ruiz Noguez entitled "Calaveras y Esqueletos Extraterrestres" (i.e. "Alien Skulls and Skeletons") states that "In reality it is a modern digital fraud a real picture of the Vietnam War. In the original photo shows the soldier with a skull, but not an alien, but a Vietcong soldier". Unfortunately, the relevant article does not cite any source for the original image nor any source for the information provided about that original image.
