### 3. The real background to this image

In fact, these photographs show the writer/actor in a science fiction movie bending over one of the props prepared for that movie.

The man bending over the "alien" shows actor and producer Paul Davids.

Paul Davids has a page on the imdb movie website at:

[http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0203194/](http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0203194/)

As shown on that webpage was the writer of the movie "Roswell" (1994) which starred Kyle MacLachlan as Jesse Marcel. Paul Davids also appears in the credits for that movie as playing a photographer:

[http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0111021/fullcredits#cast](http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0111021/fullcredits#cast)
