### 3. The real background to this video

In fact the video is edited to superimpose a "UFO" on footage of a tanker explosion in China. 5 people died in the relevant explosion. The logo on the video refers to "Section 51", which commonly appears on lists of known/probable hoaxers of UFO videos. "Section 51" is in fact is a a small team that has openly described itself online as "Internet-video creators specialized in dreaming up sci-fi, action YouTube videos, jam-packed with stunning VFX". They offer various incentives for those prepared to provide funding for their projects. For example, in return for a small fee, they offer to take the video of your choice and "we insert an original UFO on it".

The relevant tanker overturned and leaked about 20 tons of liquefied natural gas in October 2012 in Hunan province in central China. The tanker exploded, killing 5 people (including 3 fire fighters).
