*Flying Saucer Review established*

1955

, the "Flying Saucer Review" (“FSR”) magazine was established. Derek Dempster was its editor until September

1956

.

### Web Resources

There is a rapid turnover of UFO material on the Internet. Many links to material on UFO websites quickly become out of date.
