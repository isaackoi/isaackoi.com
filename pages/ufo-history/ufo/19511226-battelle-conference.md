On 26 December

1951

, Colonel S H Kirkland, of Colonel Dunn's staff, and Captain

Edward Ruppelt

left Dayton for a two-day conference to outline to Battelle Memorial Institute (referred to by Captain

Ruppelt

as “Project Bear”) the proposed project to (a) design a UFO reporting form and (b) perform a statistical study of all UFO reports.
