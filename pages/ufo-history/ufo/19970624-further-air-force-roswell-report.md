*Further Air Force Roswell Report*

On 24 June

1997

, the U.S. Air Force released a further report related to the Roswell incident, “The Roswell Report: Case Closed” prepared by James McAndrew. This report attempts to explain reports of small bodies allegedly found in conjunction with Roswell incident as test dummies.
