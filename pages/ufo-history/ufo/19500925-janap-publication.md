*JANAP 146(A)*

On 25 September

1950

, JANAP(Joint Army Navy Air Publication) 146(A) was officially issued, referring to UFOs.

JANAP 146 was first issued on 1 October [1948](/tags/1948) without any reference to UFOs.

JANAP 146 was declassified on 12 December [1953](/tags/1953).
