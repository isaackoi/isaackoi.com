*Project Cyclops design study*

During [1971](/tags/1971), a design study for Project Cyclops was prepared, co-directed by [Bernard Oliver](/ufo-history/ufo-personalities/oliver-bernard) and [John Billingham](/ufo-history/ufo-personalities/billingham-john).

“Project Cyclops: A Design Study of a System for Detecting Extraterrestrial Intelligent Life: [NASA](/ufo-history/ufo-personalities/nasa) Contractor Report 114445 (“[NASA](/ufo-history/ufo-personalities/nasa) CR 114445”)” ([1971](/tags/1971)) (available [on Amazon USA](http://www.amazon.com/dp/B000NS7YOY/?&tag=ufot-20) and [on Amazon UK](http://www.amazon.co.uk/dp/B000NS7YOY/?&tag=ufot-21)).
