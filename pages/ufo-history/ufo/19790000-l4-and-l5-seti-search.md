*L4 and L5 SETI search*

During

1979

, an attempt to discover evidence of discrete objects (such as interstellar probes) in stable orbits about L4 and L5 was made by

Robert A Freitas Junior

and Francisco Valdes by studying 90 astronomical photographical plates.
