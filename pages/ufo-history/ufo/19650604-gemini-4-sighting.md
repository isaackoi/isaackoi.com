*Gemini 4 sighting*

On 4 June

1965

, there was allegedly a UFO sighting involving Gemini 4 astronauts (including James McDivitt).

This incident is Case 27 in [Isaac Koi's "Top 100" article](/ufog/best-ufo-cases/13-the-top-100-ufo-cases), since it was referred to in 71 of the books covered by that article.

This was Project Blue Book Case Number 9448.
