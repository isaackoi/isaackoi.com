*Martian Canals*

[SETI]({{ '/tags/seti' | relative_url }}): Following observations during a particularly favourable opposition of Mars in September [1877]({{ '/tags/1877/' | relative_url }}), Giovanni Virginio Schiaparelli drew maps of Mars with lines which he referred to as “canali”. A debate ensued over the possible existence of canals on Mars.
