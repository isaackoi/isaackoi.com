*Ukraine LINCOS transmissions*

On 24 May

1999

, there was the first of a series of deliberate interstellar broadcasts from the Evpatoria Deep Space Center antenna, Ukraine by the Encounter

2001

organisation. The message for the “cosmic call”, encoded in LINCOS (“Lingua Cosmica”), was created by Yvan Dutil and Stephane Dumas.
