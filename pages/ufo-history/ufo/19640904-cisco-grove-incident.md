During the night of 4th – 5th September

1964

, Cisco Grove, California incident involving Donald Schrum (sometimes referred to as “Mr S.” or “Mr S_____”) and Vincent A ___________. Schrum claimed to have climbed a tree to avoid a robot-like creature, which he shot an arrow at.
