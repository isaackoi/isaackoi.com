On 27 October

1991

, a British television channel (Channel 4) broadcasted an Equinox documentary showing, inter alia, a circle crop being created by the

Wessex Skeptics

on land owned by farmer Martin Pitt near Marlborough, England. The circle was described by Terence Meaden as “… too hard to copy … genuine in every way” and by

Busty Taylor

as “… really a cracker”.
