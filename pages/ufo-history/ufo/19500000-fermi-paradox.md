*Fermi Paradox*

During a lunch at Los Alamos in the summer of [1950]({{ '/tags/1950' | relative_url }}), Enrico Fermi reputedly asked colleagues “If there are extraterrestrials, where are they?”. (The precise quotation and date varies from source to source.)

(Commonly referred to as “the Fermi Paradox”).
