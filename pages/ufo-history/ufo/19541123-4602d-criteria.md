*4602d criteria*

On 22 November

1954

, Colonel John M White (Commander, ATIAE-5 1st Ind) wrote to the Commander of Air Technical Intelligence Center at Wright-Patterson Air Force Base. He referred to meeting on 17 and 18 November

1954

at which, the Commander, 4602dAISS and his staff met with Captain

Charles Hardin

, ATIC and Doctor

J Allen Hynek

, for the purpose of discussing relationships in the investigation and processing of Unidentified Flying Object reports. It was agreed that ATIC would furnish the 4602d AISS with “rule of thumb” criteria to be used in investigations.
