*Wilbert B Smith memo*

On 21 November

1950

, a memo written by

Wilbert B(rockhouse) Smith

refers to “discreet enquiries through the Canadian Embassy staff in Washington” which allegedly obtained information that “flying saucers exist” and that “the matter is the most highly classified subject in the United States, rating higher even than the H-bomb”
