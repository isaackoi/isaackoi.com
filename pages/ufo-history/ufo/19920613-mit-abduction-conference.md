A five-day conference on “alien abductions” was held at Massachusetts Institute of Technology (“MIT”) between 13-17 June

1992

, co-chaired by Harvard psychiatrist John E Mack and MIT physicist David E Pritchard. Commonly referred to as “the MIT Conference”.

### Web Resources
