In Kelly-Hopkinsville, Kentucky members of the Sutton family reported strange creatures allegedly besieging their farmhouse during the night of 21 / 22 August

1955

.

This incident is Case 14 in [Isaac Koi's "Top 100" article](/ufog/best-ufo-cases/13-the-top-100-ufo-cases), since it was referred to in 92 of the books covered by that article.

This incident has featured in a list of cases produced by Greg Bishop as part of [a survey by the Fortean Times in 2007](/ufog/best-ufo-cases/9-consensus-lists-fortean-times-expert-poll-2007) of various researchers of the ten cases from 1947 onwards that interested them the most.
