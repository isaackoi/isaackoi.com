On 16 October

1957

, Antonia Villas-Boas claimed to have been abducted at about 1am, near Sao Francico de Salles in Minas Gerais, Brazil

This incident is Case 7 in [Isaac Koi's "Top 100" article](/ufog/best-ufo-cases/13-the-top-100-ufo-cases), since it was referred to in 174 of the books covered by that article.

This incident has also featured in a list of cases produced by Greg Bishop as part of

a survey by the Fortean Times in 2007

of various researchers of the ten cases from 1947 onwards that interested them the most.
