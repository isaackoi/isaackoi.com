On 2 July

1954

, a USAF F94 Starfire Jet crashed into the village of Walesville, New York, near Griffiss, killing several civilians. (Commonly referred to as “the Walesville Incident”). This crash is sometimes alleged to have occurred during the pursuit of a UFO.

This incident is Case 92 in [Isaac Koi's "Top 100" article]({{ '/ufog/best-ufo-cases/13-the-top-100-ufo-cases' | relative_url }}), since it was referred to in 30 of the books covered by that article.
