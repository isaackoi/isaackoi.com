*“The Day the Earth Stood Still”*

The [movie]({{ '/tags/movie' | relative_url }}) “*The Day the Earth Stood Still*”([1951]({{ '/tags/1951' | relative_url }})) was directed by Robert Wise.

It was based on a story by Harry Bates (“Farewell to the Master”) and starred Michael Rennie as Klaatu/Carpenter.
