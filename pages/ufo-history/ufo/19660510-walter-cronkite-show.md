On 10 May

1966

, there was a national broadcast of a news show relating to UFOs produced by the CBS News Department and narrated by

Walter Cronkite

. Most sources state the program was entitled “Flying Saucers: Friend, Foe or Fantasy?”, although some refer to “UFOs: Fact, Fiction or Fantasy”.
