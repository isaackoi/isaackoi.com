*DoD "record low"*

1959

, the US Department of Defense issues a news release entitled “Air Force UFO Study Shows ‘Unknowns’ Decreasing”, which states that the Air Force “has set a record low for the number of cases classified as ‘unknown’, which is down to less than 1%”.
