On 4 July

1947

, the United Airlines Flight 105 sighting occurred involving Captain Emil J Smith, his co-pilot (Ralph Stevens) and a stewardess (Marty Morrow), near Emmett, Idaho.

**Claims to fame**

This incident is **Case 67** in [Isaac Koi's "Top 100" article]({{ '/ufog/best-ufo-cases/13-the-top-100-ufo-cases' | relative_url }}), since it was referred to in **43** of the books covered by that article.

Isaac Koi’s [“ICES” Rating]({{ '/ufog/best-ufo-cases/27-quantitative-criteria-kois-ices-ratings' | relative_url }}) for this sighting = **5,544** (out of a potential score of 14*14*14*14 i.e. 38,416)

(1) [“Impact” Rating]({{ '/ufog/best-ufo-cases/27-quantitative-criteria-kois-ices-ratings' | relative_url }}#impact) of **12** (out of a potential score of 14), because it is **Case 67** in [Isaac Koi's "Top 100" article]({{ '/ufog/best-ufo-cases/13-the-top-100-ufo-cases' | relative_url }}).

(2) [“Credibility” Rating]({{ '/ufog/best-ufo-cases/27-quantitative-criteria-kois-ices-ratings' | relative_url }}#credibility) of **11** (out of a potential score of 14).

(2) [“Expert” Rating]({{ '/ufog/best-ufo-cases/27-quantitative-criteria-kois-ices-ratings' | relative_url }}#expert) of **7** (out of a potential score of 14). It is given this neutral rating because (at the time of writing) it has not been included in any of the short lists of the best cases produced by various UFO researchers that have been compiled by Isaac Koi (see the “[Best UFO Cases]({{ '/ufog/best-ufo-cases' | relative_url }})" article at [PART 3: Existing lists by various individuals]({{ '/ufog/best-ufo-cases/3-experts-short-lists' | relative_url }})), but also is not commonly given by UFO researchers as an example of an incident that can be explained by hoax, misperception or other mundane cause.
