On 26 October

1958

, the incident at Loch Raven Dam, Maryland, near Baltimore occurred allegedly involving Alvin Cohen and Phil Small experiencing a close encounter and engine failure.

This incident is Case 100 in [Isaac Koi's "Top 100" article](/ufog/best-ufo-cases/13-the-top-100-ufo-cases), since it was referred to in 27 of the books covered by that article.

This was Project Blue Book Case Number 6148.
