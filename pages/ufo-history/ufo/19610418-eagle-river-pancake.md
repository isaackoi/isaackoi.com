On 18 April

1961

, Joe Simonton of Eagle River, Wisconsin claims to have been provided with pancakes by occupants of a flying saucer in return for a jug of water.

This incident is Case 45 in [Isaac Koi's "Top 100" article](/ufog/best-ufo-cases/13-the-top-100-ufo-cases), since it was referred to in 57 of the books covered by that article.

This was Project Blue Book Case Number 7372.
