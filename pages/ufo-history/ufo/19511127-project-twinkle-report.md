*Project Twinkle report*

On 27 November

1951

, at the Cambridge Research Laboratory’s produces its final report on “peculiar light phenomena that had been observed in the skies of the southwestern United States”. (The relevant observations are commonly referred to as “green fireballs”).

(The relevant report is commonly referred to as “the Project Twinkle report”)
