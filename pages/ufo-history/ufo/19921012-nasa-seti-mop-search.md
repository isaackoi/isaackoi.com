*NASA SETI MOP search*

On 12 October

1992

, NASA’s SETI program (called “HRMS”, “High Resolution Microwave Survey”, or “MOP”, “Microwave Observing Project”) officially went online at Goldstone, California and Arecibo, Puerto Rico. Funds were withdrawn shortly thereafter.
