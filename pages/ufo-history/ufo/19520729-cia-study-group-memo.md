*CIA study group memo*

A memorandum from Ralph L Clark, Acting Assistant Director of the CIA’s Office of Scientific Intelligence to DDI Robert Amory, Jr, on 29 July

1952

stated that a special study group has been formed to review the situation, involving the OSI and the Office of Current Intelligence (OCI).
