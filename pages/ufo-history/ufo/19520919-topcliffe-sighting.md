*Topcliffe sighting*

On 19 September

1952

there were reports of an object seen near RAF Topcliffe, North Yorkshire, England, reportedly chasing a RAF Meteor jet piloted by Flight Lieutenant John W Kilburn during Operation Mainbrace.

This incident is Case 81 in [Isaac Koi's "Top 100" article](/ufog/best-ufo-cases/13-the-top-100-ufo-cases), since it was referred to in 34 of the books covered by that article.
