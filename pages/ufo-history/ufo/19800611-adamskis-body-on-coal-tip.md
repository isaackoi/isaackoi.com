On 11 June

1980

, the body of Zygmunt Jan Adamski was found on a coal tip in Todmorden, West Yorkshire. This has been linked by some researchers to UFO sightings in the area.

The spelling of the relevant individuals name various from source to source (e.g. – [Jenny Randles]({{ '/ufo-history/ufo-personalities/randles-jenny' | relative_url }}) says Zigmund Jan Adamski, Mantle says Zygmund Adamski, Blundell says Zygmunt).
