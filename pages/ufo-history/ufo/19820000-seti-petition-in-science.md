*SETI petition in Science*

During 1982,

Carl Sagan

publishes a petition of scientists favouring an international SETI in the journal

Science

, stating “We are unanimous in our conviction that the only significant test of the existence of extraterrestrial intelligence is an experimental one. No a priori arguments on this subject can be compelling or should be used as a substitute for an observational program”.
