On 20 May [1967]({{ '/tags/1967' | relative_url }}), Steven Michalak claims to see, and be injured by, a UFO near Falcon Lake, Canada.

This incident is Case 50 in [Isaac Koi's "Top 100" article]({{ '/ufog/best-ufo-cases/13-the-top-100-ufo-cases' | relative_url }}), since it was referred to in 54 of the books covered by that article.

This was Project Blue Book Case Number 11768.

This incident featured in a document (

“the Rockefeller Briefing Document”

) endorsed by Dr Mark Rodeghier (President of CUFOS), Richard Hall (Chairman of FUFOR) and Walter Andrus (President of MUFON) as containing “the best available evidence for the existence of UFOs”.
