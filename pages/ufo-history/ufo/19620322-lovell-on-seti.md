*Lovell on SETI*

On 24 January 1878, Congressman Emilio Q Daddario asks

Sir Bernard Lovell

(of the Jodrell Bank observatory, England) and Harrison Brown about SETI.

Sir Bernard Lovell

commented, “I think that now one has to be sympathetic about an idea which only a few years ago would have seemed rather farfetched”.
