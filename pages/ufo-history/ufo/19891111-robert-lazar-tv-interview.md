On 11 November

1989

,

Robert Lazar

was identified and interviewed on KLAS-TV in Las Vegas, having appeared in broadcasts since May

1989

under the pseudonum “Dennis”. Lazar claims to have examined the propulsion system of a UFO at Area S-4, a companion site of Area 51. (Area 51 is also commonly referred as “Dreamland”, “Paradise Ranch” and “the Groom Lake facility”.)
