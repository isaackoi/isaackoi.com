*Chadwell memo*

A further memo from H Marshal Chadwell (CIA Assistant Director for Scientifc Intelligence) on 2 October

1952

to CIA Director Walter Smith summarised his views and proposed action, including the recommendation that the Director of Central Intelligence “advise the National Security Council of the implications of the ‘flying saucer’ problem and request that research be initiated”.
