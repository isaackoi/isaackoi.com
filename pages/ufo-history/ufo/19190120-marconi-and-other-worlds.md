*Marconi and other worlds*

[SETI]({{ '/tags/seti' | relative_url }}): On 20 January [1919]({{ '/tags/1919' | relative_url }}), the New York Times prints an article entitled “Radio to Stars, Marconi’s Hope” in which Guglielmo Marconi is reported to have stated that “… communication with intelligences on other stars … may some day be possible” and that he had received unexplained signals which might have originated from the stars.
