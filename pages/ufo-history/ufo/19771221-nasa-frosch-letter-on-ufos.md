*NASA Frosch letter on UFOs*

On 21 December

1977

, a letter was written by

NASA

Administrator, Robert A Frosch, to Dr Frank Press, Director, Office of Science and Technology Policy, Executive Office of the President stating that

NASA

did not feel it could mount a UFO research effort without a better starting point than it had been able to identify thus far. The letter referred to absence of “tangible or physical” evidence for laboratory analysis.
