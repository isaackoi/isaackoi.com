On 8 January

1981

, an alleged UFO landing occurred at Trans-en-Provence, France involving Monsieur Renato Nicolai (sometimes misspelt as “Renato Niccolai”). (Monsieur Nicolai was originally referred to using the pseudonym “Renato Collini”).

This incident is Case 65 in [Isaac Koi's "Top 100" article]({{ '/ufog/best-ufo-cases/13-the-top-100-ufo-cases' | relative_url }}), since it was referred to in 45 of the books covered by that article.

This incident featured in a document ([“the Rockefeller Briefing Document”](http://web.archive.org/web/20001202173500/www.isso.org/inbox/ubd/table.htm)) endorsed by Dr Mark Rodeghier (President of CUFOS), Richard Hall (Chairman of FUFOR) and Walter Andrus (President of MUFON) as containing “the best available evidence for the existence of UFOs”.
