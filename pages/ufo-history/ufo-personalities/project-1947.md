*Project 1947*

Brief comments to be added on this group.

“[Project 1947]({{ '/ufo-history/ufo-personalities/project-1947' | relative_url }})” began in [1995]({{ '/tags/1995' | relative_url }}). It is co-ordinated by [Jan Aldrich]({{ '/ufo-history/ufo-personalities/aldrich-jan' | relative_url }}).

The primary goal of [the Project]({{ '/ufo-history/ufo-personalities/project-1947' | relative_url }}) is to search out [1947]({{ '/tags/1947' | relative_url }}) UFO reports, but as time allows it also seeks to locate records of early scientific and official interest and early UFO reports.

### Web Resources

There is a rapid turnover of UFO material on the Internet. Many links to material on UFO websites quickly become out of date. Therefore, instead of giving many links to specific webpages I have instead focused on providing tables of references to discussion of UFO incidents, personalities and other matters in various books. Those tables of references to discussion in books can be sorted by author, length and date. I hope these tools are useful.

<a id="books"></a>
### Book References

The table can be sorted by author, date and length of discussion by clicking on the relevant word in the top line of the table.

Many of the tables of references on this website are considerably longer than the references to books found on most other UFO websites. Indeed, some are probably the longest that have been collated in relation to some events and personalities. I hope that these tables are found to be a useful contribution to UFOlogy.
