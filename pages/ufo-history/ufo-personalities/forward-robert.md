*Robert Forward*

Brief comments to be added on this individual.

Robert Forward was the author of the novel “[Dragon’s Egg](/ufo-history/ufo-books/forward-robert-dragons-egg)” ([1980](/tags/1980)) (available [on Amazon USA](http://www.amazon.com/dp/034543529X/?&tag=ufot-20) and [on Amazon UK](http://www.amazon.co.uk/dp/034543529X/?&tag=ufot-21)), which considers the implications of evolution of intelligent life on the surface of a neutron star.
