*Robert Moore*

Robert Moore is a British ufologist. He has edited several UFO magazines, including one published by BUFORA.

Robert Moore is one of those individuals that are all too rare within ufology - he has done work for various UFO groups and makes high quality work available on other people's websites without publishing any books of his own or seeking any recognition.

He has a website at [http://www.deltapro.co.uk/](http://www.deltapro.co.uk/)

As discussed in the article by Robert Moore below, he is the creator of the "Primebase" database which is unusual since it tries to present a filtered sample of UK sighting reports, focusing on it does on "possible unknowns only".
