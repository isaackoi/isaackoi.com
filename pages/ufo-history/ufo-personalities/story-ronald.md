As noted in [Part 3]({{ '/ufog/best-ufo-cases/3-experts-short-lists' | relative_url }}) of Isaac Koi’s “[Best UFO Cases]({{ '/ufog/best-ufo-cases' | relative_url }})” article (i.e. in the Part entitled “[Existing lists by various individuals]({{ '/ufog/best-ufo-cases/3-experts-short-lists' | relative_url }})”), [Ronald Story]({{ '/ufo-history/ufo-personalities/story-ronald' | relative_url }}) has written an interesting book entitled “UFOs and the Limits of Science” (discussed in some detail in [PART 6: Consensus lists: Ronald Story’s poll (1979)]({{ '/ufog/best-ufo-cases/6-consensus-lists-ronald-storys-poll' | relative_url }})) which focuses around a list “of ten ‘best’ cases’”. He selected these 10 cases since they “to me represent the strongest UFO evidence on record”.

The relevant cases selected by [Ronald Story]({{ '/ufo-history/ufo-personalities/story-ronald' | relative_url }}) as the 10 “best” cases were (see Footnote 3.48):

1. [Newport News, Virginia, 14 July 1952 (Nash-Fortenberry case)]({{ '/ufo-history/ufo/19520714-nash-fortenberry-sighting' | relative_url }}) (Case 44 in [Isaac Koi's “Top 100” article]({{ '/ufog/best-ufo-cases/13-the-top-100-ufo-cases' | relative_url }}))
2. [Lakenheath AFB, England, 13-14 August 1956 (multiple radar-visual)]({{ '/ufo-history/ufo/19560813-lakenheath-incident' | relative_url }}) (Case 21 in [Isaac Koi's “Top 100” article]({{ '/ufog/best-ufo-cases/13-the-top-100-ufo-cases' | relative_url }}))
3. [Levelland, Texas, 2-3 November 1957 (Car stop cases)]({{ '/ufo-history/ufo/19571102-levelland-texas-incident' | relative_url }}) (Case 16 in [Isaac Koi's “Top 100” article]({{ '/ufog/best-ufo-cases/13-the-top-100-ufo-cases' | relative_url }}))
4. [Boianai, New Guinea, 26, 28 June 1959 (Father Gill case)]({{ '/ufo-history/ufo/19590626-father-gill-sightings' | relative_url }}) (Case 15 in [Isaac Koi's “Top 100” article]({{ '/ufog/best-ufo-cases/13-the-top-100-ufo-cases' | relative_url }}))
5. [Whitefield, New Hampshire, 19 September 1961 (Betty & Barney Hill case)]({{ '/ufo-history/ufo/19610919-hill-abduction' | relative_url }}) (Case 2 in [Isaac Koi's “Top 100” article]({{ '/ufog/best-ufo-cases/13-the-top-100-ufo-cases' | relative_url }}))
6. [Exeter, New Hampshire, 3 September 1965 (Incident at Exeter)]({{ '/ufo-history/ufo/19650903-exeter-incidents' | relative_url }}) (Case 29 in [Isaac Koi's “Top 100” article]({{ '/ufog/best-ufo-cases/13-the-top-100-ufo-cases' | relative_url }}))
7. [Ravenna, Ohio, 17 April 1966 (Spaur chase case)]({{ '/ufo-history/ufo/19660417-ravenna-police-sighting' | relative_url }}) (Case 73 in [Isaac Koi's “Top 100” article]({{ '/ufog/best-ufo-cases/13-the-top-100-ufo-cases' | relative_url }}))
8. [Mansfield, Ohio, 18 October 1973 (Coyne helicopter case)]({{ '/ufo-history/ufo/19731018-coyne-helicopter-incident' | relative_url }}) (Case 25 in [Isaac Koi's “Top 100” article]({{ '/ufog/best-ufo-cases/13-the-top-100-ufo-cases' | relative_url }}))
9. [Tehran, Iran, 18 September 1976 (Iranian jet case)]({{ '/ufo-history/ufo/19760919-tehran-jet-chase-incident' | relative_url }}) (Case 47 in [Isaac Koi's “Top 100” article]({{ '/ufog/best-ufo-cases/13-the-top-100-ufo-cases' | relative_url }}))
10. [Wellington/Kaikoura, New Zealand, 31 December 1978 (New Zealand film multiple radar-visual)]({{ '/ufo-history/ufo/19781231-wellingtonkaikoura-film' | relative_url }}) (Case 57 in [Isaac Koi's “Top 100” article]({{ '/ufog/best-ufo-cases/13-the-top-100-ufo-cases' | relative_url }}))

In addition to chapters devoted to each of the above 10 cases, [Ronald Story]({{ '/ufo-history/ufo-personalities/story-ronald' | relative_url }})’s book contains further chapters devoted to discussing various issues. A chapter entitled “UFO photographs” (see Footnote 3.49) identifies 4 photographic cases which Story states “I have chosen as the ‘best’, in terms of their combined strangeness and reliability”. Those cases are:

1. [Wellington/Kaikoura, New Zealand, 31 December 1978 (“the most puzzling picture sequence of all”)]({{ '/ufo-history/ufo/19781231-wellingtonkaikoura-film' | relative_url }}) (Case 57 in [Isaac Koi's “Top 100” article]({{ '/ufog/best-ufo-cases/13-the-top-100-ufo-cases' | relative_url }}));
2. [McMinnville, Oregon, 11 May 1950 (Trent case photos)]({{ '/ufo-history/ufo/19500511-mcminnville-photographs' | relative_url }}) (Case 17 in [Isaac Koi's “Top 100” article]({{ '/ufog/best-ufo-cases/13-the-top-100-ufo-cases' | relative_url }}));
3. [Great Falls, Mont, 5 or 15 August 1950 (Mariana film)]({{ '/ufo-history/ufo/19500815-great-falls-film' | relative_url }}) (Case 41 in [Isaac Koi's “Top 100” article]({{ '/ufog/best-ufo-cases/13-the-top-100-ufo-cases' | relative_url }}));
4. [Trindade Island, Brazil, 16 January 1958 (IGY photos)]({{ '/ufo-history/ufo/19580116-trindade-island-photos' | relative_url }}) (Case 26 in [Isaac Koi's “Top 100” article]({{ '/ufog/best-ufo-cases/13-the-top-100-ufo-cases' | relative_url }})).
